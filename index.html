RAJASTHANI's PLATE

Rajasthani food is famous for its bold and spicy flavors, with dishes like dal baati churma, gatte ki sabzi, and laal maas showcasing traditional cooking methods.

BIRIYANI

Biryani is a flavorful rice dish made with aromatic spices, meat or vegetables, and fragrant basmati rice, originating from the Indian subcontinent.

PIZZA

Pizzas are globally loved Italian flatbreads, typically topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various toppings like vegetables or meats.

SOUTH INDIAN

South Indian food is known for its rich flavors, often featuring rice, coconut, and spices, with iconic dishes like dosa, idli, and sambar.

IMARTI

Imarti is a crispy, spiral-shaped Indian dessert made from urad dal batter, soaked in sugar syrup, popular for its sweetness and crunch.
